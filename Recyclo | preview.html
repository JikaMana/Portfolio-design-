Project Overview

Recyclo is a mobile app I developed to make recycling simple, efficient, and rewarding. Users can schedule waste pickups, track their recycling habits, and earn rewards for participation. This project allowed me to strengthen my mobile development skills and contribute to an eco-friendly solution for communities.

The design focused on a clean, intuitive interface, inspired by similar apps in the recycling and sustainability space, while incorporating smooth animations and usability enhancements for a seamless user experience.
